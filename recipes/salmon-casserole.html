Salmon Casserole


Our salmon and potato bake is pure comfort food! Tender fish, a creamy light sauce, a crispy cheese topping, and plenty of leeks for that perfect hint of onion flavor.
Simply irresistible!

Ingredients

  • 600g Salmon
  • 500g Potatoes
  • 100g Spinach
  • 100g Crème légère
  • 100g Greek yogurt
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Garlic glove
  • 2 Leeks
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • Grated Cheese
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Prep the potatoes: Peel, wash, and slice them. Parboil in salted water for about 5 minutes, then drain, rinse, and let them dry.
  2. Make the sauce: Wash the spinach. Finely dice the onion and garlic, then slice the leek into rings. Sauté everything in butter for about 5 minutes. Mix crème légère and greek yogurt together until smooth and pour into the pan, bring to a boil and let it simmer for a minute. Add the spinach and let it wilt. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Assemble & bake: Rinse, pat dry, and cube the salmon. Layer the potatoes, sauce, and salmon in a baking dish. Grate cheese over the top and bake in a preheated oven (200°C conventional / 180°C fan) for about 25 minutes until golden brown.
